  1. Wash Hands Frequently:


    Washing hands will prevent the spread of germs by keeping your hands clean.

  2. Cover Faces While Sneezing and Coughing:


    To prevent the spread of germs through the air, one must cover their faces while coughing or sneezing.Use face masks and constantly sanitise hands after sneezing.

  3. Avoid Touching Face:


    Touching your face often can cause the spread of the viruses and germs into your body to increase manifold, hence it is advised to not touch your face.

  4. Social Distancing:


    It is important to maintain social distancing in public places (atleast 6 feet) to avoid spread of corona from person to person directly.
